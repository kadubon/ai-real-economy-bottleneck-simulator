# Limitations

- Aggregation hides sectoral and network heterogeneity.
- Market structure and strategic behavior are reduced-form.
- Identification may be partial under weak event variation.
- Calibration presets are illustrative, not forecasts.
- Results are scenario-sensitive and should not be interpreted as real-world predictions.
- Streamlit Community Cloud availability, hosting location, and terms are controlled by Streamlit/Snowflake.
- The simulator is not financial, investment, energy-policy, or regulatory advice.
